Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

Pulse Pop ("the App") is published by LipGen Studios. This privacy policy explains how the App handles user information.

Information Collection

Pulse Pop does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information.

The App stores gameplay preferences and local high score data only on the user's device. This information is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Pulse Pop does not currently use advertising networks, analytics services, account login systems, or external data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Pulse Pop does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, no user personal information is stored on external servers by Pulse Pop.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future if app functionality changes. Updates will be reflected on this page.

Contact

For support or privacy questions: support@lipgenerations.com
