Interactive

Not a game, not a quiz, not advice. A story with decision points that exposes what you protect when pressure shows up.

What this is

A story with pressure points

You read a real first-person story. At specific moments,the story stops and asks you to act.

Your choice won’t change the plot. It will expose the reflex you use when something is at stake.

How it works

Eight decisions,one pattern

  • 1Read normally.
  • 2Choose what you would actually do.
  • 3Finish the story.
  • 4Get a short pattern readout at the end.

No morality,no diagnosis,no “you should”. Just exposure.
