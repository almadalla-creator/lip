Adrian,Work Edition

A real story told in first person,interrupted by eight choices. Your clicks don’t change the plot,they expose the pattern you protect.

How it works

You read normally. When the story reaches a decision point,you get four options. Choose the one you would actually do,not the one you want to be.

At the end,you’ll get a short reflection: “Within the Work context,your dominant patterns were…” based only on your choices. No morality,no diagnosis.

This is built on the LIP concept: lucidity is not a label,it’s tolerance for facts without defense.
