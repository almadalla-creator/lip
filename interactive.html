Interactive

Not a game,not a quiz,not advice. A story with decision points that exposes what you protect when pressure shows up.

What Is It About

You read a real first-person story.

At specific points,you choose what you would do.

Your choice does not “change the plot”. It reveals your dominant reflex: hierarchy defense,narrative control,emotional withdrawal,or lucid tolerance.

This is a mirror,not a verdict.

Each interactive book belongs to a life territory. Work is live. Others will follow.
