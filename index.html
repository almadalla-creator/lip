What This Is About

You work hard but still feel behind.

You achieve things but it never feels stable.

You enter relationships and somehow repeat the same tension.

You believe something, yet doubt keeps returning.

That is not bad luck.

It is pattern.

LIP examines what shapes those patterns.

Not with advice. Not with inspiration. But by looking at the structures underneath your decisions, your effort and your reactions.

If something in your life feels heavier than it should, that is where to begin.
